"As contact is made with the dawn of historic times, all of Eurasia, northern Africa, and the Pacific Islands is overspread with the composite races of mankind. And these races of today have resulted from a blending and reblending of the five basic human stocks of Urantia."   81:4.1 
     
 

 
Reference
Paper 81  Development of Modern Civilization
Section 4  The Mixed Races



Editor's Note:
To 21st century ears, some terms used in this section may sound dischordant. Yet, researchers until fairly recently have used these terms to describe our biological groupings. A prevelant point-of-view is that distinguishing groups in this way is not necessary and may do harm. From Urantia studies we understand our common origins and we've learned that "blending and reblending" is the normal progression for inhabited planets. This mixing will eventually lead to a condition of minimal racial distinctions. If the revelators were communicating the papers today, perhaps they would choose different words in places to pass along identical information.
