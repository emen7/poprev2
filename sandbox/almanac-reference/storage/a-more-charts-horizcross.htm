Horizontal Cross Section
  Illustration by Bill Sadler depicting the
horizontal cross section of the master universe
from PDF: The Urantia Book Historical Society		  
 
Editor's note:  This drawing is in accord with Paper 15 which describes the east and west portions of the circuits as "comparatively straightaway."  The overall shape is based on Paper 11's description of the Isle of Paradise which sets the pattern for the creations surrounding it.  "It is definitely ellipsoid, being one-sixth longer in the north-south diameter than in the east-west diameter."

The levels are compressed to fit the schematic.  The diameter of a space level is substantially larger than the space level preceding it.
